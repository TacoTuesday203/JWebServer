Help

In order to use FreeVideo, you must copy and paste the video ID from a YouTube video into the input box. An example of a video ID is shown below. The ID is highlighted.

After pasting the video ID into the input box, you may press the Freedom! button. If you have entered a valid video ID, the video will appear and you will be able to watch it without trouble. Otherwise, you have entered an invalid video ID. Please check your ID or try a different video.
